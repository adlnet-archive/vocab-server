Welcome to the xAPI Vocabulary Registry Page. Here you can review other vocabularies and create you own vocabularies or individual terms.

Anyone is welcome to search vocabularies to find the defined meaning of a given term or see if a new term is needed.

If after considering existing terms you find that no term meets your groups needs, use your group's account or create an account and fill in the proper fields to create a new term or vocabulary.

For Phase 1, emphasis is placed on establishing the ability to create a persistent IRI which will allow the creation of vocabularyies and terms. Therefore at this point Searching is disabled, please check back in the future.
